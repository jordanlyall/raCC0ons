What is this?

Gen 0 raCC0ons are 100 pfp photos of racoons.

It's a fun experiment with CC0 media. All assets and metadata are hosted on ipfs. CC0 means they're in the public domain. You can do whatever you want. All original photos were granted CC0 license from the photographers and I have edited or enhanced them. Be on the lookout for rare raCC0ons with special traits.

Mint price = 0.08 ETH. Once all 100 are minted, you can find them on the secondary. Proceeds go to growing the community.


Minting Instructions

The only way to mint raCC0ns is via the smart contract. Here's how to do that:

  1. Visit the contract on EtherScan
  2. From the contract tab, click "Write Contract", then "Connect to Web3" and connect your wallet
  3. Expand function #2. Enter 0.08 in the payableAmount field and click "Write"
  4. Confirm the transaction in your wallet and the NFT will be minted in your wallet
  5. WARNING: If it wants you to pay a TON in gas, that means it's sold out (or you didn't enter 0.08 in the payable field)
